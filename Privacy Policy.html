Introduction

Welcome to PregnaCare. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our pregnancy care management services. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use our services.

Important: PregnaCare is committed to protecting your personal health information and maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security in accordance with healthcare regulations and best practices.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Account Information: Username, password, admin verification codes
  • Health Information: Pregnancy-related data, medical history, appointment details
  • Demographic Information: Age, date of birth, emergency contact information
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, support requests

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, browser type)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, features used)
  • IP address and general location information
  • Log files and analytics data

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Provide pregnancy care management services and features
  • Account Management: Create and manage user accounts, authenticate users
  • Communication: Send important updates, notifications, and support responses
  • Personalization: Customize your experience based on your preferences
  • Analytics: Improve our services through usage analysis and performance monitoring
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Security: Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

Authorized Disclosures

  • Healthcare Providers: With your consent, we may share information with your healthcare team
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulations
  • Emergency Situations: To protect health and safety in urgent medical situations
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales (with continued privacy protection)

HIPAA Compliance: All health information sharing complies with HIPAA regulations and requires appropriate safeguards and authorizations.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Secure database storage with encryption at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Automated backup and disaster recovery systems

Administrative Safeguards

  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Employee training on privacy and security protocols
  • Regular review and update of security policies
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Consent Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for optional data processing

How to Exercise Your Rights: Contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Provide insights for service improvement

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

Children's Privacy

PregnaCare is designed for use by adults (18 years and older) or pregnant minors with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information promptly.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications for material changes
  • Displaying prominent notices within our application

Continued use of our services after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
